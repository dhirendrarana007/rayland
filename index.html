Creators Luxury Bespoke Homes

In the most sought after Locations in Sohna of South-Gurgaon and Delhi NCR

With its foundations in Gurgaon, Sohna South of Gurgaon and New Delhi NCR, Rayland Holdings has developed and delivered multiples farm land and farmhomes projects spanning over 50 Acres plus. We have well-earned reputation for delivering beautifully designed Farmland communities in Sohna South of Gurgaon. Founded in 2018 we are a young diversified real estate company with its MBA professional founders proud to have of over 30 years combined experience in luxury property, design build, national and international sales.


From Exciting new developments to bespoke new builds and farmland conversions, we manage each project from concept to completion adding character and quality to every project that we build. From Farm homes project concept design, location, government approvals, we have the skills and expertise to design and build the ultimate luxurious and characterful new farmland habitats.
